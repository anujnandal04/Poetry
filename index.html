A Love Letter Across Timelines

On this birthday of mine… 22.
No party, no noise.
Just me.
And this room.
And somehow, you.

Maybe somewhere,
you're still talking to me at 12 a.m.,
telling me it's going to be okay,
while I trace constellations on the ceiling,
pretending they're your freckles.

About the Poet

Luca Vale is a boy made of late nights and lost words.. a quiet soul who loves too deeply and leaves too softly. He writes not to be heard, but to remember. To keep the moments that broke him beautiful.

His stories are stitched with memories, of soft smiles, missed calls, and the girl who felt like home at 2:17 A.M. Even heartbreak, in his hands, feels like a love letter.
